About me⌗

Hi! My name is Enrico and welcome to my website!

I am a CS student at University of Basilicata in Italy, I’m very passionate about all things computer science, but also music (can you guess my favorite artist? look at the website’s subtitle), movies, tv shows and motorsports.

I decided to create this blog for many reasons, I wanted to have a place to store some of my notes, but also to try and help people that want to learn more about CS by trying to unpack the more complex topics.

On this website I will also try and diversify the content of the articles by writing short essays about other topics unrelated to CS.

I hope you will find my content useful or interesting!
